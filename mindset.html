A Entrepreneur Mindset is similar to "Sigma" Mindset. They have a great self confidence, they think business oriented and they don't let negativity controll them, because they have the controll to their own life they never followed the things they been told to and they never let relationships take controll of their life and goals. A mindset that guides them to success!

I discovered that mindset back then when i was fighting with a heartbreak where i discovered the truths and reality of relationships and how many people are living unhappy working from 9 to 5 because they are living inside a reality that requires working that the goverment allows you to live.
